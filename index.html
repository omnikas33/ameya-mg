Latest updates

Important due dates

  • GSTR-1 — typically by the 11th of the following month (check your taxpayer category).
  • GSTR-3B — follow the 20th / 22nd / 24th rule as applicable to your state and turnover slab.
  • Annual returns — plan GSTR-9 / 9C timelines early; reconciliation buffers reduce last-mile risk.
