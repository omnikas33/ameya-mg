About Us

A Consultancy Built for Regulatory Confidence and Business Growth

Ameya Management Group is a Pune-headquartered consulting firm focused on GST, SEZ/STPI/EOU compliance, indirect tax advisory, ERP tax enablement, and litigation support. We partner with CFOs, finance controllers, legal teams, and plant leadership to create controls that are practical at execution level and defensible at assessment level.

Our approach combines statute interpretation, process design, and risk-based governance so clients can improve compliance outcomes without disrupting operational velocity.
